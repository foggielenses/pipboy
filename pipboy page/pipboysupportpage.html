Support and Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I reset my Pip-Boy?

A: To reset your Pip-Boy, hold down the power button for 10 seconds until the screen flickers and restarts.

Q: My Pip-Boy is not turning on, what should I do?

A: Check if the battery is fully charged. If the issue persists, try replacing the fusion core or contact our support team.

Q: How can I update the software on my Pip-Boy?

A: Connect your Pip-Boy to a terminal and run the "syst-update-pipsoft-vers-1.79" command from the settings menu. Ensure your terminal has an active network connection.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure your Pip-Boy is securely fastened to your wrist to avoid signal disruptions.
  • Regularly clean your Pip-Boy with a dry cloth to prevent dust buildup in the ports.
  • If experiencing display issues, adjust the brightness settings in the "Display" menu.
